Lab.

Three live experiments. Move your mouse across any of them.

01

Particle field

2,000 particles. Each one is pulled toward your cursor with a spring force. click to repel.

requestAnimationFrame · 2D Canvas · Verlet integration
02

Reactive shader

A single full-screen fragment shader. Coordinates warped by sin/cos and your mouse position.

WebGL · GLSL · raymarched circles
03

3D array

Sixty-four geometries arranged in a 4×4×4 cube. Drag to orbit, scroll to change the wave pattern.

Three.js · InstancedMesh · GLSL easing
All three run on a single CPU/GPU, ~60 fps on a 2018 MacBook. ← back to the cinematic experience
